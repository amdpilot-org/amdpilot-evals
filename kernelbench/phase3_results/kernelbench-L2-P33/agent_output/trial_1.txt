  The issue is that the batchnorm kernel design is flawed for this use case. For
  batch normalization during training, we need to compute mean and variance acro
  ss the batch dimension, which requires reading all M rows. This is problematic

  2. We can't have a grid dimension that depends on M for the reduction

  1. Use a 1D grid over N (features) only
